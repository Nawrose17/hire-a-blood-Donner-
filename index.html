HIRE A BLOOD DONNER

Terms and Conditions:
1. A donor can donate up to 450ml of blood at a time.
2. The donor must have a minimum hemoglobin level of 12.5 g/dL.
3. A person can donate blood every 3 months.
4. The donor should be between 18 and 60 years old.
5. Donors must weigh at least 50kg.
6. Donor must be healthy and not have any transmissible diseases.
